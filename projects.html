Advanced Methods in Power Systems (Projects)

Analysis of Voltage Sag on Transmission Systems Under Fault Conditions

In this project, the method of fault positions is applied to a power system which is spread in a geographical area of 800 kms.Using this system, performance of some selected busbars regarding voltage sags is estimated by considering both symmetrical and unsymmetrical faults.

The system was simulated in MATLAB/SIMULINK and the performance analysis of magnitude and the duration of sag was carried out by considering various power system parameters such as line length, X/R ratio and fault impedance. Also simulation results for sequence components for various types of faults were implemented in MATLAB.
